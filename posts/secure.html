Securing Your Accounts With Two Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two Factor Authenticatin (aka 2FA or Multi Factor Authentication) is a security process in which you are required to enter a second passcode when signing in to an online account. Often this second code is delivered to you via email or text message. This second step helps ensure you are the person logging in.

2FA will secure your account in the event your password is stolen or your accounts are accessed without your knowledge.

Tips

  • Apple, Facebook, Google, Amazon, LastPass, and Microsoft all have 2FA functions, but you will need to turn them on for it to work. Check the settings in your account to set them up.
  • There are several 2FA smart phone apps available that can help you consolidate 2FA access codes:
    • Google Authenticator
    • Microsoft Authenticator
    • Authy
  • If you decide to use a 2FA app, be sure to undersatnd how to access your accounts in case your phone is lost or stolen.
