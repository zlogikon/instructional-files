Password Security

About These Lessons

Passwords have long been used on the Internet to secure information like email accounts, social media profiles, and even bank accounts. Creating and maintaining secure passwords is the first line in protecting personal information on the Internt.

And yet, many people struggle with creating securing their accounts, whether they find randomized passwords difficult to create or they continually get locked out of their own accounts.

Good News! You don't have to keep repeating the same cycle of creating passwords and forgetting them, only to repeat the cycle again. With some effort up front, we can create secure passwords and organize them so that we can have confidence in the security of our online information and in our ability to access that information when needed.

In the lessons that follow, you will learn ways to organize your passwords and protect your privacy online.

These lessons have been created to be accessible on most devices. Likewise, if you prefer to print out the lessons, you can use the print command in your browser to view a printer friendly version in your preview window.
